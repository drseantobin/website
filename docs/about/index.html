About

Man fully alive.

Sean Tobin is a clinical psychologist, a convert from atheism, and a charismatic Catholic. His work sits where the interior life meets the clinic: healing, worship, and teaching people how to stay human in an age built to scatter them.

He writes The Inner Exodus, a Substack read by tens of thousands at the intersection of faith, psychology, and artificial intelligence — about attention, desire, formation, and what machines can and cannot do to the soul.

He is the author of Big God, Little Devil and Remaining Human, leads worship, and speaks widely on psychology, deliverance, and the spiritual life.

Healing

Clinical psychology in the light of grace — therapy, discernment, and the slow repair of the interior life.

Worship

Leading people into the presence of God through music — the posture the whole work flows from.

Teaching

Essays, books, and talks on faith, psychology, and the age of AI — formation for people who refuse to be automated.
