Deliverance Without Fear | Restore the Glory Podcast

Why healing begins when the heart returns to Christ

Deliverance isn’t about chasing darkness. It’s about re-centering the person in Christ.

I had the joy of sitting down with Dr. Bob Schuchts and Jake Khym on the Restore The Glory Podcast for a conversation that cuts through a lot of confusion around spiritual warfare.

We talk about how the enemy’s primary strategy isn’t dramatic possession—but fragmentation: fear, isolation, self-reliance, and disconnection. And how real deliverance is not a frantic battle against darkness, but a quiet, powerful re-integration of the human person around Jesus.

When the heart is re-centered in Christ, darkness loses its grip.

When love, trust, and praise are restored, fear collapses.

When communion replaces technique, healing accelerates.

This episode is for anyone who has felt uneasy around deliverance language—or exhausted by approaches that feel more fear-based than faith-filled. It’s a hopeful, grounded conversation about spiritual battle that’s rooted not in obsession with the enemy, but in confidence in a Father who relentlessly pursues wholeness.
